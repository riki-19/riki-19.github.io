PT. Prioritas Solusi

is a company oriented in Photo Copier Machines and Supplying Material Handling Products such as Forklift, hang pallet jack and stacker. Established in January 2005. By merging of two companies between PT. Limindo Inti Mandiri (Forklift Division) formally operated from year 2002 & PT. Ekasindo Xerox (Photo Copy Division) formally operated from year 2001, PT. Prioritas has gained all the resource and
experience to serve the best to all of our customers.

Our Vision


To be the best manufacturing company in terms of quality of the product and service, growth, profitability and environment management in the business of material handling, heavy transport equipment and fabrication component.

Our Mission
